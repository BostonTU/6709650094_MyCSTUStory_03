My Journey - Year 1

Starting my journey in CSTU in my first year was a big change from school to university. I encountered many new things, such as new friends, teachers, and more intensive course content.

Adjusting in the first year

At the beginning, I had to adjust from a structured high school education to a university education that required more self-responsibility. I not only had to understand the content in the classroom, but also had to research and learn from other sources on my own.

Subjects Learned

In my first year, I learned basic computer science subjects such as Programming Fundamentals and Data Structures, which gave me a foundation for coding and analyzing software problems.

Challenges Encountered

The challenges I encountered in my first year were learning new content and having to rely more on myself when researching and doing homework. I used to feel discouraged when I failed to code or solve a problem, but I learned not to give up. And turn to friends and teachers for help.

What I plan to do next

In the coming years, I hope to further develop my programming skills and learn new technologies related to software development. I aim to join internships and research projects in order to gain real-world experience and apply the knowledge I gain to my future work.
